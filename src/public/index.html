Operational Platform

Multi-tenant CRM, ERP, AP Portal, Financial Close, and Operational Planning — unified in a single platform built for mid-market companies.

Phase 1 — Building

Modules

Everything finance, operations, and sales teams need — integrated from day one.

☷ Quote-to-Cash

Quotes, orders, contracts, billing schedules, invoicing, AR tracking, and collections. Advance and arrears billing, milestone and usage-based, with amendment history.

⚙ Procure-to-Pay

Requisitions, purchase orders, goods receipt, and three-way matching. Approval routing with configurable thresholds and delegation.

✉ AP Portal

Email inbox ingestion, OCR via AWS Textract, confidence scoring, learned GL coding, PO matching with tolerance rules, and a queue-based processing console.

⚖ General Ledger

Double-entry posting engine with balance validation, chart of accounts, period management, journal approval workflows, and trial balance reporting.

★ CRM

Lead and opportunity tracking, account hierarchy, contact management, activity logging, pipeline visibility, and transaction-level email tracking.

⚙ Metadata Engine

Custom fields, custom transaction types, picklists, and validation rules. Tenant-configurable without code changes. The foundation everything else builds on.

⇄ Workflow Engine

State machine definitions, conditional transitions, serial/parallel approval chains, delegation, escalation, and event-driven triggers.

🔗 Integrations

BambooHR, DocuSign, and a common integration framework with field mapping, sync logging, error queues, and external key management.

📋 Audit Trail

Append-only, immutable action log. Every create, update, delete, approve, post, and reverse is recorded with before/after snapshots. No exceptions.

Core Workflows

Three end-to-end business processes, deeply integrated.

Q2C

Quote → Order → Invoice → Cash

From initial quote with e-signature through billing schedule generation, invoice delivery, AR aging, and cash receipt application.

P2P

Requisition → PO → Receipt → Invoice → Payment

From purchase request through approval, dispatch, goods receipt, AP invoice matching, coding, and GL posting.

R2R

Record → Reconcile → Close → Report

Journal entry creation, approval, posting with balance validation, period management, and financial reporting.
